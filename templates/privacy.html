Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 25, 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you use Spendly, we collect information you provide directly to us, including your account credentials and the financial transaction data you choose to record. We do not connect to external bank accounts or financial institutions.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use the information you provide solely for the purpose of delivering and improving the Spendly service. This includes displaying your expense records, generating summaries and visualizations, and maintaining the security of your account.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on our servers. We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly does not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal data to third parties. We may use trusted third-party services for hosting and infrastructure, which are bound by confidentiality obligations.

5. Cookies and Analytics

We use minimal cookies to maintain your session while logged in. We do not use third-party analytics or advertising cookies.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data at any time through your account settings. You may also request a copy of all data associated with your account.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us.
